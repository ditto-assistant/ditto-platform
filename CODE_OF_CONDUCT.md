# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

We are committed to a welcoming, safe, and productive community. Be respectful, assume good intent, give actionable feedback, and focus disagreement on ideas and evidence. Harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate disruption, and disclosure of another person's private information are not acceptable.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews, and other project spaces, as well as when representing the project publicly.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior violates these standards. Enforcement decisions should be proportionate, documented privately, and protect the safety and privacy of everyone involved.

Report conduct concerns privately to [support@heyditto.ai](mailto:support@heyditto.ai). Maintainers will review reports promptly, preserve confidentiality where possible, and avoid conflicts of interest in enforcement.
